Olympiad Competitions

Why MAB Trust? MAB Trust community About Us Our Objectives Competitions Olympiad Entries Past Papers Past Winners Quizzes Events Calendar

MAB Trust proudly hosts a series of national mathematics competitions designed to cater to students at different levels of their academic journey. We believe in challenging young minds and fostering a love for problem-solving, creativity, and ingenuity in mathematics.

We also support and participate in world-renowned international competitions, showcasing the incredible mathematical talents of Botswana's students on a global stage. These prestigious events provide students with the opportunity to represent their country, develop problem-solving skills, and network with peers from around the world, all while celebrating the beauty of mathematics.

Objectives of Mathematics Competition

  • To stimulate curiosity, enthusiasm, and love for Mathematics.
  • To expose learners to important Mathematical concepts at the school level.
  • To introduce learners to innovative techniques of problem-solving.
  • To supplement the school mathematics curriculum in line with international standards.
  • To develop mathematically gifted learners in higher-order thinking skills.
  • To foster mathematical creativity and ingenuity at the school level.
  • To provide for the satisfaction, joy, and thrill of problem-solving.
  • To prepare learners for international mathematics competitions.

Join us in our quest to uncover Botswana's brightest mathematical talents and propel them towards international success. Together, we can inspire a generation of problem solvers, critical thinkers, and STEM leaders.

PSMO, JSMO & SSMO

  • Primary Schools Mathematics Olympiad (PSMO): Open to students in Standard/Grade 5 to 7.
  • Junior Schools Mathematics Olympiad (JSMO): Open to students in Form 1 to 3.
  • Senior Schools Mathematics Olympiad (SSMO): Open to students in Form 4 to A Levels.

Mathematics Collaborative Contest (MCC)

Embark on an exhilarating journey of mathematical problem-solving with the Mathematics Collaborative Contest (MCC), a prestigious competition open to all public and private school students in Botswana. Our mission is to sharpen students' cognitive and problem-solving abilities, ignite their passion for mathematics, and create a fun and engaging learning environment.

Botswana Mathematics Festival (BMF)

Get ready to showcase your mathematical talents at the Botswana Mathematics Festival (BMF), a captivating national competition organized by MAB Trust. This event invites students from all over Botswana to participate in a variety of activities that explore the fascinating world of mathematics. Our aim is to inspire students to appreciate the beauty of mathematics, foster creativity, and promote collaboration in a lively and engaging environment.

Join Us in Our Pursuit of International Excellence

Pan African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO)

The Pan African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO) is a significant annual event that brings together talented students from across the African continent to compete in mathematics. The competition aims to foster unity among African nations, promote the pursuit of excellence in mathematics, and inspire the next generation of mathematicians. Botswana's brightest students have the opportunity to represent their country and compete with their African counterparts.

For more information, visit: https://www.africamathunion.org/AMU-pamo-official.php

International Mathematics Competition (IMC)

The International Mathematics Competition (IMC) is an annual event that brings together talented students from around the world to compete in a friendly yet challenging environment. The competition aims to inspire young mathematicians and promote a spirit of camaraderie among participants. Botswana's brightest math enthusiasts are invited to demonstrate their skills and compete for international recognition.

For more information, visit: https://chiuchang.org/imc/en/home-en/

International Mathematics and Science Olympiad (IMSO)

The International Mathematics and Science Olympiad (IMSO) is a prestigious annual event for primary school students. This competition aims to cultivate an interest in mathematics and science, foster international cooperation, and provide a platform for young talents to showcase their abilities. Botswana's most gifted students in math and science have the opportunity to represent their country and compete with their international counterparts.

For more information, visit: https://imso-official.org/

International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)

The International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) is the world's largest and most prestigious mathematics competition for high school students. This annual event challenges participants with complex mathematical problems, encouraging them to develop creative problem-solving skills and a deeper appreciation for the subject. Botswana's top-performing math students have the chance to represent their country at this elite international event and compete with the brightest young mathematicians from around the globe.

For more information, visit: https://www.imo-official.org/

MAB Trust is committed to nurturing the next generation of mathematicians by providing opportunities for students to participate in these esteemed international competitions. Our involvement in these events not only showcases the exceptional mathematical abilities of Botswana's youth but also fosters a spirit of international cooperation and understanding.
