Our Programs

Talent Identification and Mentorship

We collaborate with schools and educators across the country to identify students with exceptional potential in mathematics. Our mentorship program connects these students with experienced mentors who provide guidance, resources, and encouragement to help them excel.

Training and Development

MAB Trust offers comprehensive training programs to prepare students for national and international mathematics competitions. Our expert-led workshops and seminars equip students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to compete at the highest level.

National and International Competitions

We provide students with opportunities to participate in prestigious mathematics competitions at both national and international levels. By exposing them to challenging problem-solving scenarios, we aim to enhance their mathematical abilities and inspire them to pursue excellence in the field.

Scholarships and Financial Support

We are committed to ensuring that financial barriers do not impede any deserving student's progress. MAB Trust collaborates with generous donors and corporate partners to provide scholarships and financial aid for deserving students. This assistance helps to offset the expenses associated with competition participation, training, and other educational costs, ensuring that all talented students have the opportunity to excel in mathematics.

Community Engagement

We proactively engage with stakeholders across the nation, including various communities, educational institutions, and businesses, to raise awareness about the significance of mathematics education. By sharing success stories and celebrating achievements, we aim to inspire the next generation of mathematical leaders in Botswana and contribute to the country's continued growth in the STEM fields.

MAB Trust proudly hosts a series of national mathematics competitions designed to cater to students at different levels of their academic journey. We believe in challenging young minds and fostering a love for problem-solving, creativity, and ingenuity in mathematics.
