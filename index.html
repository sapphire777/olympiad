BRIEF INFO ABOUT

Our Trust

MAB Trust is a pioneering initiative dedicated to nurturing and promoting excellence in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through mathematics competitions. We are passionate about identifying and supporting talented young individuals, fostering their growth as they represent Botswana on international stages and become future leaders in STEM fields.

MAB Trust is committed to nurturing the next generation of mathematicians by providing opportunities for students to participate in international competitions. To showcase the exceptional mathematical abilities of Botswana's youth and to also foster a spirit of international cooperation and understanding.

Mathematics Association Botswana(MAB)

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and support the participation of talented students in local and international mathematics competitions, paving the way for a bright future in STEM fields.
